Who We Are

Curracloe Chronicles, through the medium of photographs and documents, preserves and shares events, local history and people's life stories in and around the village of Curracloe.

Where We Are

Curracloe is a seaside area, popular with holidaymakers due to its fabulous beach. It is situated approximately 10kms from Wexford town, in the sunny south-east of Ireland.
